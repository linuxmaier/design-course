## Near-decomposable subsystems

*Break your spine project into subsystems with strong internal coupling and weak coupling to each other. Name them and describe what each is responsible for.*

---

## Interfaces

*For each boundary between subsystems: what does each side need to know about the other, and what can it safely ignore?*

---

## Satisficing vs. optimising

*Where are you satisficing — accepting "good enough" because bounded rationality makes full optimisation impossible or not worth it? Where are you pretending to optimise something you actually can't?*
