# Week 7 Deliverable — Conceptual Integrity

**Week:** 7 — Conceptual Integrity
**Reading:** *The Mythical Man-Month* (selected essays) — Brooks

---

## Conceptual integrity statement

*One paragraph: the single coherent idea that everything in your system must serve.*

---

## Design audit

**What belongs?**

*Elements of the current design that serve the integrity statement.*

**What violates it and should be cut?**

*Elements that undermine or are orthogonal to the central idea.*

**Where have you compromised integrity for convenience?**

*Honest accounting of the trade-offs already made.*
